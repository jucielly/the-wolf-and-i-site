About

Developed by Team Xu, powered by the Unity technology and featuring side-scrolling gameplay with RPG elements that will keep you coming back for more.
In this epic adventure, you'll join Marja and her loyal wolf companion, Lumi, on a quest to save the world from the brink of destruction.
With intuitive controls, stunning visuals, and immersive gameplay, you'll be able to explore a rich and detailed world full of hidden secrets and challenging obstacles. But you won't be alone on your journey.
As you progress through the game, you'll unlock new abilities and powers for both Marja and Lumi, allowing you to work together in exciting and innovative ways.
Lumi can help Marja jump higher, fight enemies, and even solve puzzles by using his unique abilities. Meanwhile, Marja can protect Lumi from danger and heal him when he's hurt. With future cooperative play, you'll be able to team up with a friend and work together to take down powerful bosses and uncover hidden treasures. But even if you're playing solo, the bond between Marja and Lumi will keep you engaged and excited for every new challenge that lies ahead.
